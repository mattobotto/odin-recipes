Curry Udon

Serves one.

Source: Just One Cookbook

Description

An udon noodle soup with pork and a curry based broth.

Ingredients

  • onion(s)
  • green onion(s)
  • 200g pork loin
  • 3 cups dashi
  • 1 Tbsp sake
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 cubes Japanese curry roux (e.g., Golden Curry)
  • one serving of udon noodles (i.e., about 100g dried)

Method

Prepare

  1. Thinly slice the onion(s), green onion(s) and pork.
  2. Cook udon noodles, rinse with cold water and set aside.

Cook

  1. Add oil to a pot over "medium" heat and then add the sliced onions.
  2. Saut√© the onions for 2 minutes and then add the pork.
  3. Add the sake and dashi.
  4. Reduce heat to a simmer and periodically skim the fat.
  5. After 5 minutes, add and dissolve the curry roux cubes.
  6. Turn off the heat and add the soy sauce.

Serve

  1. Add udon noodles to a deep bowl.
  2. Ladle curry soup mixture over the noodles.
  3. Garnish with green onions.
