Shiojake

Serves four.

Source: Just One Cookbook

Description

It's salt-cured salmon.

Ingredients

  • Four portions of salmon fillets
  • 1 Tbsp sake
  • 15g salt

Method

Prepare

  1. Distribute salmon fillets evenly onto a tray and pat dry with paper towel.
  2. Sprinkle salt on all sides of each salmon fillet.
  3. Line the bottom of an airtight container with paper towel.
  4. Place salmon fillets in container, layering if necessary.
  5. Place a layer of paper towel on top of salmon fillets. Cover with lid and keep in refrigerator for two days.
  6. After two days, pat salmon fillets dry with paper towel.

Cook

  1. Broil, bake, grill or fry. It's up to you.

Serve

  1. Place fillet(s) onto a plate or some other dish of your choosing.
